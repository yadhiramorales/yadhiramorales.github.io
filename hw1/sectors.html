Sectors that GAFAM Operate In

When thinking of each of these companies, one might initially think of the Google Chrome browser for Google, Amazon for online shopping, Meta for Facebook, and the like. In reality, each of these companies have a much broader scope. Over time, they have entered several diverse areas, such as healthcare, advertising, computing, education, and beyond.

Some Sector Examples

  • Social Media and Communiation: Meta owns several communication and media platforms, such as WhatsApp, Instagram, and Facebook Messenger. Google acquired YouTube and Wazeâ€”a community-driven navigation platform.
  • IT and Technology: Amazon owns Amazon Web Services (AWS), providing cloud computing, storage, serverless platforms for any external business. Microsoft Azure plays a similar role for Microsoft. Microsoft also owns GitHub, the current industry standard for developers and open-source initiatives.
  • Health: GAFAM has several health-related aquisitions, software for medical records, wellness tools, and the like. One example is Rosetta Biosoftware, owned by Microsoft, which has expanded the capabilities of drug discovery and general biological research.
  • Gaming: Although YouTube, owned by Google, can be seen as a media platform, it also crosses into the entertainment and gaming sector. There is also Amazon with Twitch, Meta with virtual reality Oculus entertainment, and Microsoft owning Xbox and Xbox Cloud Gaming.
